## Critical Gaps Identified

### 1. **E-Commerce Integration** (CRITICAL GAP)

**Competitors Offer:**

- Wix: Full e-commerce with payment processing, inventory management, shipping
- Squarespace: Integrated e-commerce with Stripe/PayPal, product catalogs
- Shopify: Complete e-commerce platform
- 10Web: WooCommerce integration

**What We're Missing:**

- No payment processing integration (Stripe, PayPal)
- No shopping cart functionality
- No inventory management
- No order management system
- No product catalog builder
- No shipping calculator

**Impact:** E-commerce is the #1 requested feature. Without it, we lose 40%+ of potential customers.

### 2. **Real-Time Collaboration** (HIGH PRIORITY)

**Competitors Offer:**

- Framer: Real-time team collaboration
- Webflow: Team workspaces with permissions
- B12: Human + AI collaboration workflow

**What We're Missing:**

- No team collaboration features
- No shared editing sessions
- No role-based permissions
- No comments/annotations system
- No version control for teams

**Impact:** Enterprise and agency customers require collaboration. This limits our market to solo users.

### 3. **Advanced Analytics & A/B Testing** (HIGH PRIORITY)

**Competitors Offer:**

- Squarespace: Real-time analytics, conversion tracking
- Wix: Advanced analytics dashboard, A/B testing
- GoDaddy: Marketing analytics, conversion optimization

**What We're Missing:**

- Basic analytics only (mentioned but not implemented)
- No A/B testing capabilities
- No conversion funnel tracking
- No heatmap analysis
- No user behavior tracking
- No performance monitoring dashboard

**Impact:** Businesses need data to optimize. Without analytics, we're just a builder, not a growth platform.

### 4. **Template Library & Design System** (MEDIUM PRIORITY)

**Competitors Offer:**

- Wix: 800+ templates
- Squarespace: 230+ premium templates
- Framer: Modern, interactive templates
- Webflow: Extensive template marketplace

**What We're Missing:**

- No template library
- No pre-built design systems
- No template marketplace
- Limited design variety (AI-generated only)

**Impact:** Users want to start from proven designs. AI-only generation feels risky.

### 5. **Marketing Automation** (MEDIUM PRIORITY)

**Competitors Offer:**

- GoDaddy Airo: Full marketing suite (email, social, ads)
- B12: CRM integration, email marketing
- Durable: Automated marketing campaigns

**What We're Missing:**

- No email marketing integration
- No social media scheduling
- No automated campaigns
- No CRM integration
- No lead capture automation

**Impact:** Small businesses need marketing tools. We're only solving website creation, not business growth.

### 6. **Mobile App Builder** (EMERGING GAP)

**Competitors Offer:**

- Wix: Mobile app builder (new feature)
- Some platforms: Progressive Web App (PWA) generation

**What We're Missing:**

- No mobile app generation
- No PWA features
- Limited mobile optimization focus

**Impact:** Mobile-first businesses need apps. This is the future.

### 7. **Advanced SEO Tools** (MEDIUM PRIORITY)

**Competitors Offer:**

- 10Web: Real-time SEO analysis, automated optimization
- Squarespace: Advanced SEO tools, schema markup
- Wix: SEO Wiz, automated optimization

**What We're Missing:**

- Basic SEO strategy only
- No real-time SEO scoring
- No automated schema markup generation
- No SEO audit tools
- No backlink analysis
- Limited keyword research depth

**Impact:** SEO is critical for visibility. Our investigation is good but needs ongoing optimization.

### 8. **Third-Party Integrations** (MEDIUM PRIORITY)

**Competitors Offer:**

- Wix: 500+ app integrations
- Squarespace: Extensive integrations
- 10Web: WordPress plugin ecosystem

**What We're Missing:**

- No integration marketplace
- No Zapier/Make.com integration
- No CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- No email service integrations (Mailchimp, SendGrid)
- No social media integrations
- No calendar/booking integrations

**Impact:** Businesses use multiple tools. Without integrations, we're isolated.

### 9. **Content Management System (CMS)** (MEDIUM PRIORITY)

**Competitors Offer:**

- 10Web: WordPress CMS
- Webflow: Built-in CMS
- Squarespace: Content management features

**What We're Missing:**

- No blog/content management
- No dynamic content editing
- No content scheduling
- No media library management
- Static sites only

**Impact:** Businesses need to update content regularly. Static sites become outdated.

### 10. **Performance & Hosting Features** (LOW PRIORITY - We're Good)

**Competitors Offer:**

- Global CDN (we have this)
- SSL certificates (we have this)
- Custom domains (we have this)

**What We're Missing:**

- No performance optimization dashboard
- No caching strategies
- No image optimization automation
- No lazy loading implementation
