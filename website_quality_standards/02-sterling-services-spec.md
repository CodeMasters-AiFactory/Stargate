### 4-6 FAQs

#### Q1: How do you structure fees for different services?
**A:** "Fee structures vary by service type and complexity. Corporate matters may be billed hourly or on a project basis. Family law matters are typically hourly with retainer arrangements. Criminal defense may be hourly or fixed-fee depending on the matter. We provide clear fee estimates during consultation."

#### Q2: Do you offer initial consultations for all services?
**A:** "Yes, we offer confidential initial consultations for all practice areas. Consultations typically last 30-60 minutes and allow us to understand your situation, explain your options, and outline how we can help. There is no obligation to proceed."

#### Q3: How quickly can you start work on my matter?
**A:** "Response times vary by urgency. Criminal matters require immediate attention, and we can often act within hours. Corporate and family law matters typically begin within 1-3 business days after engagement. We'll discuss timelines during your consultation."

#### Q4: Can you handle matters outside [CITY]?
**A:** "We primarily serve clients in [CITY] and [REGION], but can assist with matters in other jurisdictions where appropriate. Some matters may require local representation, which we can arrange or refer. We'll discuss jurisdiction during your consultation."

#### Q5: What information should I bring to my consultation?
**A:** "Bring any relevant documents (contracts, court papers, correspondence), a timeline of events, and a list of questions. For corporate matters, bring company documents. For family law, bring relevant financial and personal documents. For criminal matters, bring any charge sheets or court documents."

#### Q6: Do you work with other professionals (accountants, mediators)?
**A:** "Yes, we often collaborate with accountants, mediators, private investigators and other professionals to provide comprehensive support. We can recommend trusted professionals or work with your existing team."
