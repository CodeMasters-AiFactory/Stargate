## Image Specifications

### Technical Requirements
- **Format:** WebP (preferred) or JPEG
- **Resolution:** 
  - Hero images: 1920x1080px or larger
  - Service cards: 800x600px
  - Team photos: 400x400px (square)
- **Optimization:** Compressed for web, < 200KB per image
- **Alt Text:** Descriptive, SEO-friendly alt text for each image

### Brand Colour Application
- **Primary:** Navy blue (#1e3a8a or similar)
- **Accent:** Copper/amber (#d97706 or #b45309)
- Images should incorporate these colours subtly
- Avoid clashing colours or overly bright palettes

### Style Guidelines
- Professional, not playful
- Modern but timeless
- South African context where applicable
- No visible text in images
- High quality, not generic stock photos

---

## Image Generation Workflow

1. **Generate images** using DALL·E or available image generator
2. **Review for brand alignment** (colours, style, professionalism)
3. **Optimize for web** (compress, convert to WebP if possible)
4. **Add descriptive alt text** for SEO and accessibility
5. **Test on different devices** to ensure quality
6. **Update prompts** based on results for future use

---

## Notes for Future Refinement

- Test different image styles to find what resonates
- Consider custom illustrations vs. photography
- Ensure images don't slow down page load
- Use lazy loading for below-fold images
- Consider image CDN for better performance
