# Quick Start

Get up and running with Stargate Portal in under 5 minutes.

## TL;DR

```bash
git clone https://github.com/CodeMasters-AiFactory/Stargate.git
cd Stargate
npm install
npm run dev
```

Open [http://localhost:5000](http://localhost:5000) and start building!

## Your First Website in 60 Seconds

### 1. Launch the Wizard

Click **"Start Building"** on the homepage to launch the Merlin Wizard.

### 2. Describe Your Business

Tell Merlin about your business:

> "I'm building a website for a modern coffee shop called Bean & Brew in Cape Town. We specialize in single-origin coffee and artisan pastries."

### 3. Choose a Package

Select your tier:
- **Starter** - Basic 3-page website
- **Professional** - 5-page with SEO optimization  
- **Enterprise** - Full-featured with e-commerce

### 4. Pick a Template

Browse templates by industry or let AI recommend one based on your description.

### 5. Customize with AI

Use the visual editor to tweak your site. Ask Merlin for help:
- "Change the hero section background to a warm coffee image"
- "Add a menu section with our products"
- "Make the contact form send to info@beandbrew.co.za"

### 6. Generate Images

Click the Leonardo AI button to generate custom images:
- "A cozy coffee shop interior with warm lighting"
- "Artisan latte art on a wooden table"

### 7. Preview & Deploy

Preview your site on desktop, tablet, and mobile views, then deploy!
